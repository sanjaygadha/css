what is the history of bcci

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) was set up on December 4, 1928 by a group of players at Delhi's Roshnara Club to end the British monopoly in cricket.

why bcci has 3 stars

The three stars embroidered on the team's jersey symbolise India's victories in three World Cups so far.
